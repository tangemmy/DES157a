White UNIQLO Japan-Exclusive Dress

So I learned a couple things.
1. Uniqlos in Japan have different items than ones in the US.
2. Japanese uniqlo only hold S,M,L sizes in-store, smaller/larger sizes are online only- I needed an XS.
3. Dish soap can remove oil stains.
Buying this online was a struggle, I had to input a Japanese billing address... so I ended up just put my Japanese school's and it worked! Then I picked it up at the local Lawsons and it was in an unexpectedly large My Hero Academia box. I wore this out for the first time to the Gion Matsuri in Kyoto and ate the best wagyu skewers in my life only to find out I dripped oil all over and then proceeded to scrub it out with dishsoap at the Air BnB.
